About the Program

What is Made In Alaska?

What is Made In Alaska?

Made In Alaska is a state-administered program that allows qualifying Alaska-made products to use the official mark. On Voyij, we highlight eligible products so you can shop with confidence.

How do products qualify?

Eligibility is tied to program requirements and an active permit. Products shown here are flagged as eligible and associated with a permit verified as active by the Alaska Division of Economic Development.

Active permit status

Permit status can be refreshed automatically against the state registry. If a permit expires, items are removed from this collection while remaining available on Voyij under the store's normal listings.
